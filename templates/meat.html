Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  2. Add sliced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add mushrooms and continue cooking until they release their juices.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the beef mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Slowly pour in beef broth, stirring constantly until sauce thickens.
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in sour cream. Cook until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve over cooked egg noodles or rice.
Beef Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, beef broth, and chili powder.
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions if desired.
Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, marinate beef slices in soy sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add marinated beef slices and stir-fry until browned.
  4. Add sliced bell pepper, broccoli florets, and sliced carrots. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice.
Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill taco shells with seasoned beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, sour cream, and cilantro.
  5. Squeeze lime juice over tacos before serving.
