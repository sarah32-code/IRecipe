Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and season with minced garlic, salt, pepper, and dried dill.
  3. Place lemon slices on top of salmon fillets.
  4. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Serve hot with steamed vegetables or rice.
Pan-Seared Halibut

Ingredients:

  • 4 halibut fillets
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season halibut fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add halibut fillets to the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice over halibut fillets and sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.
Baked Cod with Herbs

Ingredients:

  • 4 cod fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place cod fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped parsley, dried oregano, and dried thyme.
  4. Brush herb mixture over cod fillets and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve hot with roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.
Spicy Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, paprika, cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
  4. Warm tortillas in the skillet for 30 seconds on each side.
  5. Assemble tacos by placing shrimp, shredded cabbage, avocado slices, and chopped cilantro on tortillas.
  6. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over tacos.
