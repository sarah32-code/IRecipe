Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. In a shallow dish, mix breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Place flour and beaten eggs in separate shallow dishes.
  4. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Place coated chicken breasts in a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through.
  6. Remove from oven, top each chicken breast with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Return to oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Sprinkle with chopped fresh basil before serving.
Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour marinade over them, turning to coat.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove chicken breasts from marinade and discard excess marinade.
  6. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through and juices run clear.
  7. Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
Honey Garlic Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced carrots

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sliced chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until chicken is browned and cooked through.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, and cornstarch.
  4. Pour sauce over chicken in the skillet and stir to coat.
  5. Add broccoli florets and sliced carrots to the skillet and cook until vegetables are tender-crisp and sauce has thickened.
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
Baked Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. In a small saucepan, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Cook over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.
  3. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and water until smooth. Stir cornstarch mixture into the saucepan and cook until sauce thickens.
  4. Place chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour teriyaki sauce over them, turning to coat.
  5. Bake chicken for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and vegetables.
